        Ibrahim Rouza located in Bijapur is a must-visit if you are touring Karnataka. 
        Ibrahim Adil Shah is said to have built this beautiful monument dedicated to 
        Taj Sultana, his queen. The Islamic architecture of then, it is one of the 
        daintiest monuments in the history of Karnataka that emanates austere charisma.
